How to keep your knives sharp:

  1. Only hand wash your knives. The dishwasher is very hard on delicate knife edges.
  2. When using your knives be sure to use a cutting board. A wooden board is recommended.
  3. Store your knives in a safe place, such as a knife block, cardboard covers, or magnetic holder.
  4. Using a steel on your knives will help maintain to edge. There are many websites showing the proper use of a steel.

How to keep you scissors sharp:

  1. Only use fabric shears on fabric. Paper or other hard materials may damage your scissors edge.
  2. Keep your scissors clean and dry.
  3. Store your scissors in a safe place.
  4. Scissors covers can be purchased at most fabric stores. Covers can help to protect the cutting edges of your scissors.

How to keep your garden tools sharp:

  1. Keep your tools clean and dry. Basic tool oil, such as 3 in 1 oil, will keep your tools clean and moving well.
  2. Store your tools in a safe place. Store your tools in such a way that they won’t be exposed to other tools or easily damaged by other tools.
  3. When using your mower blade try to stay away from rocks, sticks, and landscaping. This will help to maintain the edge on your blade.
